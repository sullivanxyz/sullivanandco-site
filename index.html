Built for Us First

Every system below started as something I built for my own family. These aren't screenshots — they're working demos. Click around.

01 · FlagshipReal project

Wedding Guest Rolodex

Every guest, every table, every relationship — one living list. Pick a table on the left, then tap guests in the web to see their card. Dashed circles are open seats.

The original ran a real wedding — six months of RSVPs, one source of truth. It still runs the holiday-card list today.

02 · Sample data

The Family Budget, Reframed

Not another finance app. Three buckets, a plan mark on every bar, and an honest sentence about the month. Flip between months; open a bucket to see the lines.

Built on the tools you already have — no new app, no new logins, aligned to your goals.

03 · Sample data

Newborn Log

Feeds, diapers, sleep — one tap to log at 3am, a clear pattern by breakfast. Try the buttons; the stats and the 24-hour strip update live.

Designed for one-handed use in the dark, shared between both parents' phones.

04 · ConceptBuilt to order

The Family Domain

Your household with its own address. Shared inboxes and lists that end the forwarding chains. Tap an address to see who it reaches.

This one's a teaser — every family's setup is different.
